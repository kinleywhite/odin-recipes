Quinoa Boal

Description:

This healthy and light dinner packed with protein will surely become one of your weekly recipe staples!

Indgredients:

  • Quinoa
  • Kale
  • Zucchini
  • Artichoke Heart
  • Grape Tomato
  • Pecans
  • Balsamic Glaze
  • Avocado

Instructions:

  1. Cook the quinoa.
  2. In a pan, sautee the kale, zucchini, grape tomatoes, kalamata olives, and pecans until golden.
  3. Assemble quinoa and sauteed veggies in a bowl, top with avocado and drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  4. Enjoy!
